Rider Activity

This interactive dashboard shows how ride activity changes throughout the week and throughout the day. I've also included a bar chart comparing each weekday to see which days see the most traffic

During the week, we can see rider activity peaks twice around 8 AM and 6 PM, which aligns to people's work schedules. On weekends, rides tend to peak only once later in the day, around 10 AM, then slowly decreases thorughout the day. We can also see that the most popular days to ride are Wednesdays while weekends seem to be the least popular.

Membership Breakdown

When I saw the data for the first time, the membership column immediatly caught my attention. I wanted to compare the data between members and non members. How do non members use these bikes compared to their member counterparts?

In the Membership Breakdown dashboard, we can see that non members tend to ride longer distances than members do, but members are riding more than 3 times as many rides than non members. We can also see a difference in bicycle preference between the two groups. Non members seem to prefer electric bikes while members prefer to ride classic bikes.

Popular Station Map

This map was a requirement from our assignment. It is meant to show The most popular Citi bike stations used in New York. I highligted popular bike stations in orange and provided a bar chart sorting each station by popularity. Its interesting to see how many more rides start in the top 3 stations compared to the rest.
